Your Thrive Assessment

A 3D model of your life — across 10 domains, 4 dimensions, and one honest conversation with yourself. Takes about 5 minutes.

Step 1 of 4 · Value Ranking · ~45 seconds
What matters most to you?
Not all life domains carry the same weight. A devoted parent and an ambitious entrepreneur will rank these very differently — and both are right. Drag to rank the 10 domains from most to least important in your life right now. #1 = what matters most to your sense of a meaningful life.
    Step 2 of 4 · Satisfaction & Direction · ~2 minutes
    How are you doing — honestly?
    This is your moment to check in — not to perform, not to compare. Listen to your inner voice. How fulfilled do you genuinely feel in each area right now? And: is it getting better or worse? A 4 is not a failure — it is simply a place that wants your love.
    Step 3 of 4 · Readiness · ~60 seconds
    Where will you invest this season?
    We have limited resources — time, energy, attention, money. This is not about giving up on any area of life. It is about being strategically honest about where you can meaningfully invest right now. Trying to work on everything leads to progress on nothing.
